Frequently Asked Questions

Peptides are short chains of amino acids linked by peptide bonds. They serve as important tools in biochemical and pharmacological research, enabling scientists to study receptor binding, cell signaling pathways, and structure-activity relationships.

All VitaRevive products are sold strictly for in-vitro research and laboratory use. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, nor for use in diagnostic or therapeutic procedures. Purchasers assume responsibility for compliance with all applicable regulations.

Each compound undergoes rigorous quality control. We provide Certificates of Analysis (COA) with every order, including HPLC purity data and mass spectrometry confirmation. Our standard purity threshold is 98% or higher.

Lyophilized peptides should be stored at -20 degrees Celsius in a dry environment, protected from light. Once reconstituted, peptides should be aliquoted and stored at -20 degrees Celsius to minimize freeze-thaw degradation. Detailed handling instructions are included with each shipment.
